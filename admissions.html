Admission Procedure

All the candidates appearing either for Entrance Examination or Direct admission have to submit duly filled admission
form with attested photo copies of testimonials. Original testimonials are to be shown at the time of admission. The reservations
of seats are as per the Hon’ble Supreme Court’s decision. The selection of candidates for admission to any
course in university seat (50% of the total) and payment seats (35% of the total) is made on the merit list prepared on the basis
of the Entrance Test conducted by the college on all India basis as per University orders. 15% seats in all the courses are reserved
for NRI/ Management/ Sponsored candidates. There will be no Entrance Test in this category and Direct Admission procedure is followed.
Any seat which remains vacant after expiry of stipulated times, given to a candidate on waiting list will automatically revert back to
the general category. The final selection is done after conducting the interview/counseling of the candidate by College Admission Committee.

NOTE:-

Candidates applying from Jammu & Kashmir and Nepal, have to submit the original
document of police verification issued by the SSP or District Police Authority.
Fees once deposited will not be refunded in any case.
